Coma in Algiers

Happy Forever

Even though I try to find music on Bandcamp, the amount of stuff you have to crawl through makes finding artists that interest you difficult. Especially when they have "noise" as a tag.

I'm really digging this album. I really like how in your face the opening track "For What" is. It gets the album off to a great start with a noisy, metallic riff that sets the tone for the rest of the album. There's a consistent groove running throughout the songs that reminds me of "Slip It In" era Black Flag, especially in "Freeland" and "Let's Get Married.

I know I say that basically every rock album I review has "Swans" as an influence but the song "Swansea" sounds like someone listened to "Filth" and wanted to copy the style, which it does pretty well.

"Leipzig" ends the album very suddenly, and left me wanting a more solid closing track. Despite being four minutes long I feel like it ended too suddenly.

In any case this is a rare gem coming out of Bandcamp and I recommend it if you're a fan of noise rock or punk rock.

Favorite track: Sexual Beings

Least Favorite Track: 95
