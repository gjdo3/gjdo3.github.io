What music player I use, and why

I use Clementine. It has smart playlists, support for fetching track information, and plugins for last.fm, spotify, etc, if you use those services. It's the best graphical music player available on OS X and Linux, in my opinion, and probably the best music player on Windows (tied with foobar2k, but foobar isn't free as in freedom, so I don't use it.)
